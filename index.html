Profile

I am a full stack developer with 32 years of development experience. I hold a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Information Systems from Missouri State University. I started writing code at the age of 6 in QBasic. It was an era before Windows 3.1, where 5.25 and 3.5 floppy disks abounded. DOS 5.0 had just released, a mouse had significantly less value, and learning new technologies required a trip to the local library.

Since then, I have developed in numerous languages and frameworks for organizations of all sizes. I've been around long enough to have learned and forgotten far more than I can remember but that experience developed me to a point where I can learn and adapt new technologies in a matter of days, not months. When you've spent as much time developing as I have, you begin to understand that every language and framework is simply a tool. The real skill is understanding, defining, and solving the problem in the most efficient and effective way possible.
