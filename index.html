Profile

I am a seasoned full stack developer with over three decades of professional experience. Holding a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Information Systems from Missouri State University, I embarked on my coding journey at the age of six with QBasic—during an era before Windows 3.1, when 5.25" and 3.5" floppy disks were ubiquitous, DOS 5.0 had just been released, and learning new technologies necessitated trips to the local library.

Throughout my career, I have developed software in a myriad of languages and frameworks for organizations of all sizes. My extensive experience has allowed me to learn and adapt to new technologies swiftly, often within days rather than months. After years in the field, I’ve realized that each language and framework is merely a tool. The true expertise lies in comprehending, defining, and resolving problems in the most efficient and effective manner possible.
